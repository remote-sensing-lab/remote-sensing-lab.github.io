About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Geography at Binghamton University, State University of New York (纽约州立大学-宾汉姆顿). My research focuses on GIScience, remote sensing, and environmental modeling. I use various geospatial techniques to study wetland ecology, coastal geomorphology, and regional hydrology. Currently, I am serving as an Associate Editor for WETLANDS and Editorial Board member for REMOTE SENSING. More information about my research can be found at http://wetlands.io
